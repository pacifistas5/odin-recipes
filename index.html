Japanese Recipes

Japanese cuisines are famous for inventing and innovating, this happens a lot in Japanese cuisine. The Japanese unbelievably enjoy and turn everything into food! It seems like every restaurant wants to be totally different from one another. While we are used to always seeing the same pizza and pizza flavors in Brazil, the Japanese go further and invent sakura (flowers) sodas, black hamburgers, bread with noodles, tea ice cream and thousands of other things that we have never seen in our lives.
